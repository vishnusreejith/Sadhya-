An Interactive Culinary Journey

Sadhya Etiquette & Layout

The banana leaf is placed with its tapering end to the left. Dishes are served on the top half, starting with pickles on the far left. The main course, rice, occupies the bottom center. This arrangement ensures a balanced tasting experience, moving from tangy starters to savory curries and finally to the sweet dessert.
